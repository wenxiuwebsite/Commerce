2018 Chinese New Year Gala Highlights

Celebrating the Year of the Dog with the CCCWA community

← Back to News
Event 2018

The Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) welcomed the Year of the Dog with its 2018 Chinese New Year Gala — one of the organization's flagship annual events and a celebration that brought together hundreds of members, partners, and guests from across the Greater Seattle area.

The evening was a vibrant showcase of Chinese culture, community achievement, and the growing presence of Chinese-American businesses in Washington State. Held at a premier Seattle venue, the gala combined traditional celebration with modern ceremony, reflecting the dual identity of a community that bridges two cultures.

Performances and Entertainment

The gala featured a program of cultural performances highlighting classical Chinese artistry alongside contemporary talent. Traditional Chinese dance, instrumental performances, and vocal acts drew enthusiastic applause from the assembled guests. The performances were organized to reflect the spirit of the new year — vibrant, hopeful, and celebratory.

The evening's program was carefully curated to appeal to both Chinese-speaking members and English-speaking guests, making the event an accessible introduction to Chinese cultural traditions for attendees from all backgrounds.

Gala Banquet and Networking

A multi-course gala banquet gave members and guests the opportunity to enjoy traditional Chinese cuisine while networking with peers from across Washington State's Chinese-American business community. The banquet reflected the chamber's commitment to creating space for genuine connection — beyond formal business settings — that strengthens the bonds within the community.

The event brought together entrepreneurs, investors, legal and financial professionals, technology executives, and community leaders, creating one of the most diverse gatherings in CCCWA's calendar year.

Award Presentations

A highlight of the gala was the annual award presentation, recognizing outstanding contributions to the CCCWA community by individual members and partner organizations. Awards acknowledged excellence across categories including business leadership, community service, cultural contribution, and support for CCCWA's mission of bridging US-China commerce and culture.

The 2018 gala marked another successful chapter in CCCWA's tradition of annual celebrations that build community pride and strengthen the organization's identity as a cornerstone of Chinese-American business life in the Pacific Northwest.
