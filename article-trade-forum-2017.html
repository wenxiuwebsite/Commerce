China Province & Washington State Trade and Investment Cooperation Forum

中国省与华盛顿州贸易投资合作论坛 — July 26, 2017

← Back to News
Community July 26, 2017

On July 26, 2017, the Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) hosted one of its most significant events to date: the China Province and Washington State Trade and Investment Cooperation Forum (中国省与华盛顿州贸易投资合作论坛). The forum brought together high-level delegations from multiple Chinese provincial governments with Washington State business leaders, investment professionals, and government representatives to explore concrete opportunities for bilateral cooperation.

The event represented a key milestone in CCCWA's mission to facilitate cross-border trade and investment, demonstrating the organization's growing role as a bridge between regional actors on both sides of the Pacific.

Delegates and Participants

The forum drew provincial delegations representing several of China's most economically active regions, each bringing specific investment priorities and sector interests. On the Washington State side, participants included business leaders from technology, real estate, agriculture, healthcare, and financial services — sectors in which the Pacific Northwest has both depth and global standing.

Government representatives from Washington State participated alongside private sector figures, signaling the official recognition of CCCWA's convening role and the broader significance of China-Washington economic ties to the state's economic development strategy.

Discussion and Outcomes

Forum sessions focused on identifying specific areas of mutual interest for investment and trade development. Real estate and infrastructure development, technology and innovation, agricultural exports, and tourism were among the sectors explored. Participants discussed both the opportunities and the practical challenges of cross-border investment, including regulatory requirements, cultural differences, and due diligence practices.

One-on-one meetings between Chinese provincial representatives and Washington State businesses were arranged alongside the main forum sessions, allowing for targeted conversations that could lead to specific partnerships and transactions. CCCWA played a direct facilitation role, connecting parties based on aligned interests and providing bilingual support throughout the process.

Significance for CCCWA

The forum demonstrated CCCWA's capacity to organize substantive, high-level engagements that go beyond networking to produce real economic opportunity. By hosting provincial delegations alongside Washington State counterparts, CCCWA positioned itself as an indispensable intermediary in the growing economic relationship between China's regions and the Pacific Northwest — a role the organization has continued to develop through subsequent forums and delegation visits.
