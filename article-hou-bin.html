Paralympic Ambassador Hou Bin Keynote

Promoting the Olympic Spirit — Perseverance and the Pursuit of Dreams

← Back to News
Event July 2018

The Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) hosted a keynote address by Hou Bin (侯斌), the renowned Chinese Paralympic champion who was named an image ambassador for the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics and the International Paralympic Committee. The event — the 18th installment of CCCWA's popular "Seattle Lecture Series" (西雅图大讲坛) — drew a packed audience of members and community guests.

Hou Bin's story is one of extraordinary determination. A high-altitude jump athlete who lost his leg in an accident as a young man, he went on to win Paralympic gold medals and became one of China's most celebrated disabled athletes. His journey — from injury and adversity to Olympic glory and international ambassadorship — made him an ideal speaker for CCCWA's community, many of whom had built their own lives and businesses across cultural and personal barriers.

The Keynote: Promoting the Olympic Spirit

Titled "Promoting the Olympic Spirit, Strengthening Perseverance, and Pursuing Dreams" (弘扬奥运精神，坚定毅力，追求梦想), Hou Bin's keynote drew on his personal experience as an elite athlete and a public figure representing China on the world stage. He spoke about the values that had sustained him through recovery and competition: discipline, resilience, the refusal to be defined by circumstance.

For members of the Chinese business community in Seattle — many of whom had immigrated, built companies from scratch, and navigated the challenges of operating across two very different business cultures — Hou Bin's message carried a direct resonance. The qualities that make a champion athlete, he argued, are the same qualities that make a community thrive: commitment, teamwork, and the long view.

International Cooperation and the 2022 Beijing Olympics

As an ambassador for the upcoming Beijing 2022 Winter Games, Hou Bin also spoke about the significance of the Olympics as a platform for international connection and understanding. He discussed the role that events like the Games — and organizations like CCCWA — play in building genuine bridges between China and the rest of the world, beyond trade and politics, into shared human experience.

The event was one of the most attended in CCCWA's lecture series history, reflecting both the appeal of Hou Bin as a public figure and the community's hunger for inspiring stories from the broader Chinese and Chinese-American world.

About the Seattle Lecture Series

CCCWA's Seattle Lecture Series (西雅图大讲坛) brings expert speakers to the community on a rotating basis, covering topics from business and law to science, culture, and public affairs. Launched in early 2017, the series has become one of CCCWA's most valued member programs, offering substantive, bilingual programming that connects the Seattle Chinese-American community to ideas and expertise from across both countries.
