Why Join CCCWA?

Membership in the Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State connects you with a growing network of entrepreneurs, investors, and business leaders bridging the US and China.

As a member you receive access to exclusive events, bilingual business resources, legal and consulting referrals, and opportunities for cross-border trade partnerships.

Corporate Membership For registered businesses — full access to all CCCWA programs, events, and directory listing
Individual Membership For entrepreneurs and professionals — networking access and event invitations
Associate Membership For community supporters — newsletter, select events, and community updates
