About CCCWA

The Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) is a nonprofit corporation established pursuant to the Washington Nonprofit Corporation Act for mutual benefit.

We promote and facilitate investment and trade cooperation between China and the United States, working with organizations across Washington State on matters of mutual interest.
