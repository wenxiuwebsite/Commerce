Publish Changes

After making changes in the News and Events panels, export a data.js file and place it in the content/ folder of your site. The site will automatically use it to display your updated content.

1
Export your content
Click the button below to download data.js. This file contains all your news and events.
2
Replace the content file
Place the downloaded data.js into the content/ folder of your website, replacing the existing file.
3
Deploy to Netlify
If hosting on Netlify, upload or push the updated content/data.js to your repository. Netlify will automatically publish the changes. For local testing, just refresh the page in your browser.
