Seattle Opera Night — Concert by Young Chinese Vocalists
CCCWA presented an Opera Night in Seattle featuring young Chinese vocalists performing classical works in a live concert. The event was a celebration of Chinese musical talent and an opportunity for the community to experience world-class performance in an intimate setting.

PRC National Day Celebration Banquet
CCCWA hosted a formal banquet celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China, bringing together members, consular representatives, business leaders, and community guests to mark the occasion with ceremony, food, and a shared sense of cultural pride.

August 2016
Announcement
CCCWA Now Seeking New Members — Join Washington State's Chinese Chamber
CCCWA announced its inaugural membership drive, inviting Chinese-owned businesses and professionals across Washington State to join the newly established chamber. The announcement outlined membership benefits, eligibility, and how to apply — marking the formal launch of the organization's community outreach.
Aug 2016
Event
Alibaba Technology Seminar in Seattle
CCCWA co-organized a technology seminar in Seattle featuring Alibaba — exploring e-commerce platforms, cloud computing through Alibaba Cloud, and business opportunities for US companies looking to access Chinese consumers and for Chinese businesses expanding internationally.

July 2016
Community
Baidu President Yaqin Zhang Visits Seattle — Dialogue with the Pacific Northwest
CCCWA helped facilitate a visit to Seattle by Baidu President Yaqin Zhang and VP of HR Hui Liu, enabling dialogue between China's leading search and AI company and the Pacific Northwest's technology and business community — a signal of Seattle's growing importance in global tech.
Jul 28, 2016
June 2016
Announcement
CCCWA Official Opening Ceremony
The Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) held its official opening ceremony in June 2016, marking the founding of Washington State's dedicated Chinese business chamber. The ceremony brought together founding members, community leaders, and diplomatic representatives to mark the launch of an organization built to bridge US-China commerce in the Pacific Northwest.
Jun 2016
Community
Hainan Airlines Receives First Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner
CCCWA celebrated Hainan Airlines' delivery of the first Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner — a milestone reinforcing the deep aviation and manufacturing ties between China and the Pacific Northwest, home to Boeing's primary production facilities. The event highlighted the economic relationship that connects Washington State to China's growing aviation industry.
