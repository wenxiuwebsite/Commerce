CCCWA Celebrates 20th Anniversary of Hong Kong SAR

Announcement July 2017

In July 2017, the Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) joined Chinese communities around the world in marking the 20th anniversary of the establishment of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (HKSAR). The July 1, 1997 handover — returning Hong Kong to Chinese sovereignty under the "one country, two systems" framework — was one of the defining moments of modern Chinese history, and its 20th anniversary was observed with celebrations on every continent.

CCCWA organized a celebratory event for its members and the broader Chinese-American community in Washington State, creating a space to reflect on the significance of the anniversary and to celebrate the cultural, commercial, and historical ties that connect the Pacific Northwest's Chinese-American community to Hong Kong.

Hong Kong's Role in US-China Commerce

For CCCWA's membership — businesses and professionals engaged in cross-border trade, investment, and commerce between China and the United States — Hong Kong holds particular significance. As one of the world's leading international financial centers and a major hub for US-China trade flows, Hong Kong has long served as a critical gateway for Chinese businesses entering global markets and for international companies establishing a foothold in Greater China.

Many of CCCWA's members have business relationships with Hong Kong, whether through banking, shipping, legal services, or direct investment. The anniversary celebration was therefore not purely cultural — it was also an acknowledgment of Hong Kong's enduring importance to the international business networks that CCCWA's members navigate.

A Moment for the Community

Beyond its commercial dimensions, the celebration provided the community with an opportunity to reflect on a broader historical narrative — the story of modern China's emergence as a global power, its relationship with the diaspora communities that carry Chinese culture and values into cities like Seattle, and the aspirations of a generation that came of age watching Hong Kong's unique trajectory.

CCCWA brought together members representing diverse backgrounds across mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and the broader diaspora, reflecting the organization's inclusive vision of community that transcends regional boundaries in favor of shared values and mutual support.

The event was a reminder that CCCWA's work is not only about business — it is about maintaining the cultural and historical connections that give the Chinese-American community in Washington State its depth, its richness, and its sense of shared belonging.
