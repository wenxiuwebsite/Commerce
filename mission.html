About Us

The Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) is a nonprofit corporation established pursuant to the Washington Nonprofit Corporation Act for mutual benefit.

Our Objectives

  • To represent Chinese enterprises and entrepreneurs in the State of Washington;
  • To provide forums in which Chinese businesses in the State of Washington can identify and discuss common interests regarding their commercial interests in the United States;
  • To work with other organizations in the State of Washington on matters of mutual interest and to enhance bilateral communication and collaboration between Chinese and American business communities;
  • To promote Chinese investment into and China-bound investment from the State of Washington and to develop and facilitate USA-China cross-border trade and commerce;
  • To do all other lawful things as may be incidental or conducive to the attainment of the foregoing objectives.

Our Values

CCCWA is committed to integrity, inclusiveness, and excellence in serving its members and the broader community. We believe that strong business communities are built on trust, collaboration, and mutual respect across cultural boundaries.
